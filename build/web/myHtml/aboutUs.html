About Us

Welcome to CITIZEN SOLVE dedicated to fostering better communication between citizens and the government. Our platform provides a seamless way for residents of any city or village to report and address major issues affecting their communities. Whether it's a broken road, a malfunctioning streetlight, or any other concern, citizens can easily upload their complaints through our user-friendly interface.
At our core, we believe in the power of direct communication to drive positive change. By empowering citizens to voice their concerns directly to the government, we aim to expedite the resolution of pressing issues and enhance the overall quality of life in our communities.
Our platform serves as an official channel for citizens to highlight significant problems and for the government to take prompt action. Through surveys and assessments conducted in the respective areas, we ensure that reported issues are thoroughly evaluated and addressed in a timely manner.
Key Features
1. Easy Problem Submission: Users can conveniently upload complaints about major problems they encounter in their area, whether it's through text, photos.
2. Government Engagement: We facilitate direct engagement between citizens and the government, enabling officials to address reported issues effectively.
Join us in building stronger, more responsive communities. Together, let's make a difference one problem at a time.
